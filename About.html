GDG Constantine

GDG Constantine is a vibrant community of developers in the city of Constantine, dedicated to exploring and leveraging Google technologies. The community serves as a platform for developers to come together, share knowledge, and engage in discussions about the latest advancements in the tech industry.

What is GDG ?

Google Developer Groups (GDG) aspire to be the most helpful way for developers to connect with others to learn about and build successfully on all Google platforms. There are more than 1,000 GDG communities in more than 145 countries/regions around the world. Google Developer Groups is the world's largest developer community network. Each GDG is a local community center for professional developers who share their expertise and passion for Google developer technologies.
