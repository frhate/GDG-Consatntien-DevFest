DevFest

DevFests are local technology workshops hosted by the global Google Developer Group (GDG). Each DevFest event is hosted by local sponsors to match the needs and interests of the local developer community. Whether it's through hands-on learning experiences, technical talks from experts in local languages, or simply meeting with other local developers, DevFest attendees learn how to collaborate and innovate together through Google's developer tools.
